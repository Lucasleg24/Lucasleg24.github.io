FAQ

Can I play the games even if I have problems holding a controller?
No problem, everything is doable with your hands, so there is never any need for a controller to play our games.
Does the game offer tutorials to learn how to solve the Rubiks Cube?
Yes, Rubikscape includes a comprehensive tutorial mode that teaches you step-by-step how to solve the Rubik's Cube, from basic movements to advanced techniques.
Can you play without an Internet connection?
Absolutely! Rubikscape is designed to be fully playable offline. Once downloaded, you can enjoy all features without needing an internet connection.
Does the game allow tracking progress and improving solve times?
Yes, Rubikscape includes a detailed statistics system that tracks your solve times, move counts, and personal bests, helping you monitor your progress and improvement over time.
